Our Mission

Capturing Moments, Creating Stories

Welcome to the Lens & Light Photography Contest 2025. We believe that photography is not just about capturing moments, but about seeing the world through different perspectives and telling compelling stories through visual art.

Whether it's the serene calm of nature, the raw emotions captured in a portrait, or the vibrant energy of street life - every photograph tells a unique story. Our contest provides a platform for students to express their creativity and share their vision with the community.

Theme: "Nature Through the Lens"

Explore and capture the beauty of the natural world in three distinct categories

Landscapes

Capture sweeping vistas, majestic mountains, dramatic skies, and the grandeur of natural scenery. Show us the world's beauty through wide-angle perspectives.

Wildlife

Document the fascinating world of animals in their natural habitats. Capture behavior, emotion, and the untamed spirit of wildlife in stunning detail.

Macro Details

Explore the intricate details of flowers, insects, textures, and the hidden beauty that exists in the smallest elements of nature's design.
