Frequently Asked Questions

The contest is open to all students from every department at Shri Ramswaroop Memorial University. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced photographer, we welcome your participation!

You can use any camera, including smartphones, point-and-shoot cameras, DSLRs, or mirrorless cameras. What matters is your creativity and vision, not the equipment!

Each participant can submit a maximum of 3 photographs. Make sure each photo aligns with the "Nature Through the Lens" theme and falls under one of the three categories: Landscapes, Wildlife, or Macro Details.

Basic editing such as color correction, cropping, and minor adjustments are allowed. However, heavy manipulation that alters the reality of the scene is not permitted.

Winners will be announced on December 1st, 2025. The results will be posted on our website and communicated via email to all participants.
